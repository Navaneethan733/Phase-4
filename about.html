Our Journey

Milestones in our growth and development

2010

STACKLY Biotech was founded by Dr. Elena Rodriguez with a vision to revolutionize genomic medicine.

2013

Launched our first product, GeneSeq Basic, establishing our presence in the genomic sequencing market.

2015

Expanded research facilities and established partnerships with three major research universities.

2017

Received FDA approval for our first diagnostic kit, marking our entry into clinical applications.

2019

Opened European headquarters in Berlin, expanding our global reach and research collaborations.

2021

Launched our breakthrough immunotherapy platform, advancing cancer treatment options.

2023

Celebrated our 500th research publication and expanded into stem cell therapeutics.
