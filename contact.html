Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

What is your typical response time for inquiries?

We strive to respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days. For urgent matters, we recommend calling our main line or using the emergency support number for after-hours issues.

Do you offer international shipping for your products?

Yes, we ship our products worldwide. Shipping times and costs vary by location. Our sales team can provide specific details for your country, including any import regulations or restrictions that may apply.

Can I schedule an on-site demonstration of your equipment?

Absolutely! We offer both virtual and on-site demonstrations of our equipment. Please contact our sales department to schedule a demonstration at your facility or arrange a visit to one of our demonstration centers.

What type of support do you provide after purchase?

We provide comprehensive post-purchase support including installation, training, technical support, and maintenance services. All our products come with warranty coverage, and we offer various service contracts for ongoing support.

Do you offer custom solutions or modifications to your products?

Yes, we frequently work with clients to develop custom solutions or modify our standard products to meet specific research or clinical needs. Please contact our technical team to discuss your requirements.
